## Industry Terminology

### Financial Product Development
- **Product-Market Fit (PMF)** - Validation that a financial product meets genuine market demand
- **Regulatory Sandbox** - Testing environment with relaxed regulatory requirements for innovation
- **Time-to-Market (TTM)** - Duration from product concept to customer availability
- **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** - Simplest version with core financial functionality
- **Digital-First Strategy** - Prioritizing digital channels over traditional banking methods

### Banking Technology
- **API Gateway** - Central point managing external access to banking services
- **Core Banking System** - Central system processing daily banking transactions
- **Omnichannel Experience** - Consistent customer experience across all platforms
- **Open Banking** - APIs allowing third-party access to banking data and services
- **Fintech Stack** - Technology infrastructure supporting financial technology services

### Compliance & Risk
- **Know Your Customer (KYC)** - Identity verification and due diligence processes
- **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)** - Systems preventing money laundering activities
- **Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)** - Security requirements for card processing
- **Basel III** - International regulatory framework for banking supervision
- **Regulatory Technology (RegTech)** - Technology solutions for regulatory compliance

### Development Methodologies
- **DevSecOps** - Development practices integrating security throughout the process
- **Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)** - Automated development and deployment pipelines
- **Feature Flags** - Conditional activation of new features for controlled rollouts
- **Blue-Green Deployment** - Production deployment strategy minimizing downtime
- **Microservices Architecture** - Distributed system design with independent service components
