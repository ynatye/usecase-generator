# Building Materials × Product & R&D
## monday.com SE Playbook

### Industry Context

The building materials industry operates at the intersection of performance engineering, regulatory compliance, and sustainability innovation. Product & R&D teams are under intense pressure to develop materials that meet increasingly stringent building codes, environmental standards, and performance requirements while reducing time-to-market. These teams manage complex workflows spanning material formulation, ASTM/ISO testing protocols, third-party certifications (UL, GREENGUARD, ENERGY STAR), and sustainability documentation (EPDs, HPDs, Declare labels).

The regulatory landscape is particularly challenging, with teams needing to track compliance across multiple jurisdictions, building codes (IBC, IRC, NFPA), and green building standards (LEED v4.1, BREEAM, Living Building Challenge). R&D cycles can span 18-36 months from initial formulation to market launch, involving iterative testing phases, pilot manufacturing runs, and extensive documentation for CSI MasterFormat specifications. The rise of digital construction workflows has also created demand for BIM objects, parametric models, and digital material passports that integrate with design software.

Modern building materials R&D departments are increasingly focused on circular economy principles, bio-based materials, and carbon reduction strategies. Teams must balance traditional performance metrics (compressive strength, fire ratings, thermal performance) with new sustainability KPIs like embodied carbon, recyclability, and end-of-life disposal. This complexity demands sophisticated project management and data coordination capabilities that traditional tools struggle to provide.

### Department Profile
- **Typical Team Size:** 15-45 professionals (material scientists, engineers, regulatory specialists, sustainability experts)
- **Key Stakeholders:** R&D Director, Materials Engineers, Lab Technicians, Regulatory Affairs, Product Marketing, Manufacturing Engineering, Quality Assurance
- **Core KPIs:** Time-to-market, Testing success rate, Regulatory approval timeline, Innovation pipeline value, Sustainability score improvements, Patent applications filed
- **Common Tools Replaced:** Excel spreadsheets, Sharepoint, LIMS systems, Asana/Jira, email chains, manual testing logs
