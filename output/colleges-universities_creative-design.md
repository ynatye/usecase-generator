## Industry Terminology

### Creative & Design Terms
- **Brand Guidelines** - Official standards for logo usage, colors, typography, and messaging
- **Style Guide** - Comprehensive document outlining visual and written communication standards
- **DAM (Digital Asset Management)** - System for organizing, storing, and distributing digital assets
- **Creative Brief** - Document outlining project requirements, objectives, and constraints
- **Comp/Mock-up** - Visual representation of design before final production
- **Proof** - Final review version requiring approval before production
- **Bleed/Trim** - Printing terminology for areas beyond final cut lines
- **Raster vs Vector** - Image file types (pixel-based vs mathematical graphics)
- **CMYK vs RGB** - Color models for print vs digital applications
- **Kerning/Leading** - Typography spacing adjustments

### Higher Education Terms
- **Academic Calendar** - Schedule of semesters, breaks, and important academic dates
- **Enrollment Cycle** - Annual process of recruiting, admitting, and registering students
- **Yield Rate** - Percentage of admitted students who actually enroll
- **FAFSA** - Free Application for Federal Student Aid
- **CRM (Constituent Relationship Management)** - System for managing relationships with students, alumni, donors
- **SIS (Student Information System)** - Database containing student academic and personal records
- **Advancement** - Fundraising and development department
- **Athletics Conference** - Group of institutions competing in organized sports
- **NCAA Compliance** - Adherence to National Collegiate Athletic Association regulations
- **Accreditation** - Official recognition that institution meets educational standards

### Marketing & Communications Terms
- **Integrated Marketing** - Coordinated approach across multiple channels and touchpoints
- **Attribution** - Tracking which marketing activities lead to desired outcomes
- **A/B Testing** - Comparing two versions to determine which performs better
- **Conversion Rate** - Percentage of audience that takes desired action
- **Engagement Rate** - Measure of audience interaction with content
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** - Average cost to acquire one new student/donor
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)** - Total value a student/donor provides over relationship duration
- **Organic Reach** - Audience reached without paid promotion
- **Earned Media** - Publicity gained through promotional efforts other than paid advertising
- **Thought Leadership** - Positioning institution as authority in specific areas
