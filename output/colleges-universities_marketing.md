## Use Case 1: Enrollment Marketing Campaign Orchestration

### Pain
Marketing teams juggle 15+ simultaneous enrollment campaigns (undergrad, grad, international, transfers) across departments, each with different timelines, budgets, creative assets, and success metrics. Campaigns miss deadlines, budgets overlap, creative gets duplicated, and ROI tracking is impossible.

### Solution
**monday.com Work Management + CRM + AI Agents**
- Unified campaign dashboard with automated task dependencies
- Real-time budget tracking with alerts and approvals
- Asset library with version control and brand guidelines enforcement
- Lead scoring and nurture automation based on engagement
- AI-powered campaign optimization recommendations

### Outcome
- 40% faster campaign launch times
- 60% reduction in duplicate creative work
- 25% improvement in cost-per-lead across all programs
- Real-time visibility into pipeline health by program type

### Discovery Questions
1. "How many enrollment campaigns are you running simultaneously right now?"
2. "What percentage of your campaigns launch on time and on budget?"
3. "How do you currently track which creative assets perform best across different audience segments?"
4. "When a prospect shows interest in multiple programs, how do you coordinate the handoff?"
5. "How long does it take to get approval for campaign changes or budget adjustments?"

### Industry Context
Higher ed marketing operates in cycles (fall/spring recruitment, summer orientation, etc.) with strict deadlines that can't move. Missing a campaign launch by even a week can cost thousands of lost prospects. Teams often work in silos (undergrad, grad, continuing ed) with different CRMs and processes.

### Vibe Prompt
*"You're the Director of Marketing at a mid-size private university. It's March, you're launching fall enrollment campaigns, and your team is drowning. Undergrad marketing is using Salesforce, grad programs are on HubSpot, international recruitment is in spreadsheets, and your creative team has 47 versions of the viewbook across different folders. Show me how monday.com becomes your single source of truth."*

### Agent Blueprint
**Campaign Orchestrator Agent:**
- Monitors campaign timelines and sends proactive alerts
- Automatically creates subtasks when new programs are added
- Flags budget variances and suggests reallocation
- Generates weekly executive reports with enrollment funnel health
- Integrates with admissions systems to track conversion rates

## Industry Terminology

**AACRAO** - American Association of Collegiate Registrars and Admissions Officers
**CRM** - Customer/Constituent Relationship Management (students, alumni, donors)
**EAB** - Education Advisory Board (research and consulting)
**FTE** - Full-Time Equivalent (student enrollment measurement)
**FAFSA** - Free Application for Federal Student Aid
**GE/Gen Ed** - General Education requirements
**IPEDS** - Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System
**NACAC** - National Association for College Admission Counseling
**NSLC** - National Student Leadership Conference
**ROI/ROAS** - Return on Investment/Return on Ad Spend
**SLAC** - Small Liberal Arts College
**SEM** - Strategic Enrollment Management
**Title IV** - Federal financial aid programs
**Yield Rate** - Percentage of admitted students who enroll
