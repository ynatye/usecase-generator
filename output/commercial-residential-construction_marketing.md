## Industry Terminology

### Marketing-Specific Terms
- **Spec Home**: Speculation/inventory home built without a specific buyer
- **Model Home**: Furnished display home showcasing builder quality and options
- **Grand Opening**: Major marketing event for new developments or model homes
- **Broker Tour**: Exclusive preview events for real estate professionals
- **VIP Preview**: Early access events for preferred customers/prospects
- **Hard Hat Tour**: Construction site visits for prospects during building phase
- **Design Center**: Showroom for selecting finishes, fixtures, and upgrades
- **Trade Show Circuit**: Annual rotation of major construction industry events

### Construction Marketing Context
- **Punch List Marketing**: Last-minute marketing touches before project completion
- **Turnkey Marketing**: Complete marketing packages for entire developments
- **Co-op Marketing**: Shared marketing costs/efforts with trade partners
- **CAM Charges**: Common Area Maintenance charges affecting commercial marketing costs
- **Marketing Premium**: Price increase justified by superior marketing/branding

### Digital & Content Terms
- **Virtual Tours**: 3D/360° digital walkthroughs of properties
- **Drone Footage**: Aerial photography/videography of projects and sites
- **Time-Lapse**: Accelerated video showing construction progress
- **Rendering**: Computer-generated visualization of planned projects
- **BIM Marketing**: Using Building Information Modeling for marketing materials
