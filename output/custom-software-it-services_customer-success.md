## Industry Terminology

**Key Terms:**
- **Implementation Success Rate:** Percentage of projects delivered on time, on budget, and meeting client requirements
- **Time-to-Value (TTV):** Duration from contract signing to client realizing measurable business value
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** Percentage of revenue retained and expanded from existing clients year-over-year
- **Client Health Score:** Composite metric including usage, satisfaction, engagement, and financial health
- **Technical Debt:** Accumulated shortcuts or suboptimal solutions that impact future development efficiency
- **Scope Creep:** Uncontrolled expansion of project requirements beyond original specifications
- **SLA Compliance:** Adherence to service level agreements for response times and resolution targets
- **Adoption Metrics:** Measurement of client user engagement and feature utilization
- **Expansion Revenue:** Additional revenue from existing clients through upsells, cross-sells, or increased usage
- **Churn Prevention Window:** Time period during which proactive intervention can prevent client loss

**Industry Acronyms:**
- **CSM:** Customer Success Manager
- **SLA/SLO:** Service Level Agreement/Objective  
- **NPS/CSAT:** Net Promoter Score/Customer Satisfaction
- **ARR/MRR:** Annual/Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **CAC/LTV:** Customer Acquisition Cost/Lifetime Value
- **QBR:** Quarterly Business Review
- **UAT:** User Acceptance Testing
- **MVP:** Minimum Viable Product
- **API:** Application Programming Interface
- **DevOps:** Development Operations
