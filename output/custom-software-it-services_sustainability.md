# monday.com SE Playbook: Custom Software & IT Services × Sustainability

## Executive Summary

This playbook targets Custom Software & IT Services companies implementing sustainability initiatives, ESG compliance, and green technology practices. Companies in this vertical face unique challenges balancing technological innovation with environmental responsibility, requiring integrated platforms to manage carbon footprint tracking, ESG reporting, sustainable procurement, and regulatory compliance.

## Three Core Value Drivers

### 1. Replace or Radically Augment Headcount
- **Automate ESG data collection and reporting** reducing 2-3 sustainability analyst FTEs
- **AI-powered carbon footprint calculations** replacing manual tracking teams
- **Automated compliance monitoring** reducing regulatory affairs staffing by 40-60%

### 2. Consolidate Tech Stack with AI
- **Replace 4-8 disconnected tools** (carbon tracking, ESG dashboards, compliance systems, procurement tools)
- **Unified sustainability data model** eliminating data silos between departments
- **AI-driven insights** consolidating multiple analytics platforms into one intelligent system

### 3. Scale Impact Without Overhead
- **Scale ESG reporting across global offices** without proportional staff increases
- **Expand green software practices** across development teams without dedicated sustainability engineers
- **Grow B Corp/certification compliance** without expanding compliance teams

## Industry Terminology

### ESG & Compliance
- **Scope 1/2/3 Emissions**: Direct, indirect energy, and value chain emissions
- **CSRD**: Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (EU)
- **TCFD**: Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures
- **EU AI Act**: Comprehensive AI regulation framework
- **Science-Based Targets (SBTi)**: Emission reduction goals aligned with climate science
- **Carbon Intensity**: Emissions per unit of revenue or output
- **PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness)**: Data center energy efficiency metric

### Green Technology
- **Carbon-Aware Computing**: Optimizing workloads based on grid carbon intensity
- **Green Software Foundation**: Industry standards for sustainable software development
- **Energy Proportionality**: Matching computing resource usage to actual demand
- **Software Carbon Intensity**: Carbon emissions per functional unit of software
- **Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)**: Certificates representing renewable energy generation

### Business Impact
- **ESG Score**: Third-party sustainability rating (MSCI, Sustainalytics)
- **Impact Measurement**: Quantifying positive social/environmental outcomes
- **Materiality Assessment**: Identifying most significant ESG issues for stakeholders
- **Stakeholder Capitalism**: Business model prioritizing all stakeholders, not just shareholders
- **Shared Value**: Creating economic value while addressing social/environmental challenges
