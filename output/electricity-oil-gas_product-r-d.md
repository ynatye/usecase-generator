## Industry Terminology

### Core R&D Terms
- **TRL (Technology Readiness Level)** - 1-9 scale measuring technology maturity
- **LCOE (Levelized Cost of Energy)** - Economic metric for comparing energy technologies
- **Capacity Factor** - Actual energy output vs. maximum potential output
- **Energy Density** - Energy stored per unit mass or volume
- **Round-trip Efficiency** - Energy recovered vs. energy input for storage systems
- **Power-to-X** - Technologies converting renewable electricity to other energy carriers

### Battery & Storage Specific
- **C-rate** - Rate of discharge relative to battery capacity
- **SOC (State of Charge)** - Current charge level as percentage of capacity
- **Cycle Life** - Number of charge/discharge cycles before performance degradation
- **Calendar Life** - Operational lifetime under normal conditions
- **DoD (Depth of Discharge)** - Percentage of capacity discharged

### Grid & Smart Energy
- **Grid Code** - Technical requirements for connecting to electrical grid
- **Ancillary Services** - Services that maintain grid stability and power quality
- **Demand Response** - Adjusting energy consumption based on grid conditions
- **Prosumer** - Entity that both produces and consumes energy
- **Virtual Power Plant** - Network of distributed energy resources

### Oil & Gas R&D
- **EOR (Enhanced Oil Recovery)** - Techniques to extract additional oil from reservoirs
- **EUR (Estimated Ultimate Recovery)** - Total recoverable reserves from a reservoir
- **Permeability** - Measure of rock's ability to transmit fluids
- **Fracturing** - Creating fractures in rock to increase oil/gas flow
- **Subsurface Modeling** - Creating digital representations of underground formations
