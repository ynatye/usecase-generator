# Financial Software × Marketing
## monday.com SE Playbook

### Industry Context

Financial software marketing operates in one of the most heavily regulated environments, where every claim must be substantiated and every piece of content requires compliance review. Marketing teams in fintech face the unique challenge of communicating complex financial concepts while adhering to FINRA, SEC, and state banking regulations. The industry is experiencing rapid transformation with embedded finance, open banking APIs, and Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS) platforms creating new market categories and competitive dynamics.

Marketing departments in financial software companies must navigate lengthy content approval workflows, with legal and compliance teams reviewing everything from website copy to conference presentations. The highly competitive landscape includes well-funded competitors ranging from traditional banks to nimble fintech startups, requiring sophisticated ABM campaigns targeting specific financial institutions, credit unions, and banking platforms. Content marketing focuses heavily on thought leadership around topics like embedded finance positioning, regulatory technology, and financial inclusion.

The marketing mix typically includes B2B campaigns targeting banks and financial institutions, partner co-marketing with established banking platforms, and sometimes B2C efforts for direct-to-consumer financial products. Major industry events like Money20/20, Finovate, LendIt, and regional banking conferences require significant coordination and compliance review, while digital marketing must carefully navigate advertising restrictions and fair lending language requirements.

### Department Profile
- **Typical Team Size:** 8-25 marketers (varies by company stage and market focus)
- **Key Stakeholders:** CMO, Legal/Compliance, Product Management, Sales, Partnership teams, Regulatory Affairs
- **Core KPIs:** MQL/SQL conversion, pipeline attribution, partner-sourced leads, compliance approval cycle time, content velocity, event ROI, banking customer acquisition cost
- **Common Tools Replaced:** Asana/Trello for workflows, Smartsheet for compliance tracking, multiple spreadsheets for campaign management, email chains for approval processes, separate project tools for events/conferences

**Key Boards/Workflows:** Content Calendar board with compliance status columns, Legal Review Pipeline with SLA tracking, Claims Substantiation board linking to supporting documentation, Content Archive with regulatory approval history.

**Vibe Prompt:** "Create a content marketing board for fintech that tracks compliance review stages, legal approvals, and regulatory substantiation requirements with automated workflows for FINRA advertising review"

**Agent Blueprint:** Compliance Content Agent that monitors content through approval stages, automatically flags potential regulatory issues, assigns reviewers based on content type, and maintains audit trails for all marketing materials with regulatory approval status.

**Value Driver:** Consolidate Tech Stack with AI

**vs. Enterprise Solutions (Smartsheet, MS Project):** monday.com offers the visual simplicity and collaboration features that complex enterprise tools often lack, while still providing the workflow automation and compliance tracking that fintech marketing teams need. Better user adoption and faster implementation.

**vs. Compliance-Specific Tools:** Purpose-built compliance tools handle regulatory workflows but don't integrate with marketing campaign execution, content creation, and cross-team collaboration. monday.com consolidates the entire marketing operation while maintaining compliance capabilities.
