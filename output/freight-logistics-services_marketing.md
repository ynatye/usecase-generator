## Industry Terminology

| Term | Definition | Marketing Relevance |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **3PL (Third-Party Logistics)** | Companies providing logistics services to shippers | Primary target market for marketing campaigns |
| **4PL (Fourth-Party Logistics)** | Integrators managing entire supply chains | High-value prospects requiring complex solutions |
| **Freight Broker** | Intermediary connecting shippers with carriers | Key partnership channel for lead generation |
| **Lane Density** | Freight volume on specific routes | Critical for campaign targeting and pricing |
| **LTL (Less Than Truckload)** | Shipments smaller than full truck capacity | Specific service marketing category |
| **TL/FTL (Truckload/Full Truckload)** | Shipments filling entire truck capacity | High-value service requiring specialized marketing |
| **Deadhead** | Empty truck movement without cargo | Cost factor affecting pricing and campaigns |
| **Shipper** | Company sending freight | Primary customer target for all campaigns |
| **Carrier** | Company transporting freight | Recruitment target for capacity building |
| **Spot Market** | On-demand freight pricing | Volatile pricing requiring dynamic campaigns |
| **Contract Rate** | Negotiated long-term pricing | Stable revenue source for retention campaigns |
| **Freight Class** | Standardized shipping categories (50-500) | Targeting criteria for specialized campaigns |
| **FMCSA** | Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration | Compliance requirement affecting carrier recruitment |
| **MC Number** | Motor Carrier authority number | Required identifier for broker partnerships |
| **Load-to-Truck Ratio** | Market supply/demand indicator | Key metric for campaign optimization |
