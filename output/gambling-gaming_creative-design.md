# Gambling & Gaming × Creative & Design Playbook

## Overview

Creative & Design departments within gambling & gaming companies are responsible for creating the visual identity and user experience across every customer touchpoint — from casino floor layout design and slot machine cabinet art to sportsbook UI/UX design and iGaming app visual design. These teams typically range from 15-50 creatives in regional operators to 200+ in major gaming conglomerates like MGM, Caesars, or DraftKings. They work under strict regulatory oversight, managing brand consistency across physical venues, digital platforms, and promotional materials while adhering to responsible gaming visual communications standards.

The department structure usually includes specialized sub-teams: environmental designers for casino floor layouts and VIP lounge design concepts, digital designers for iGaming platforms and sportsbook interfaces, brand managers for gaming brand identity and loyalty program branding, and marketing creatives for slot tournament promotional materials and social media content. These teams must balance creative innovation with regulatory compliance, manage complex approval workflows, and coordinate with architecture firms for hotel/resort visual identity and entertainment venue design projects.

The regulatory environment demands meticulous documentation, version control, and approval tracking for all creative assets — from table felt/layout design to responsible gaming messaging. Teams must also manage seasonal campaigns, real-time promotional content for digital displays, and rapid-turnaround social media content while maintaining brand consistency across hundreds of touchpoints from player card designs to food & beverage outlet branding.
