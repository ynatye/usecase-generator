# monday.com SE Playbook: HR & Staffing × Creative & Design

## Executive Summary

This playbook targets HR & Staffing firms that provide creative and design services to their clients, focusing on three core value drivers: **Replace or Radically Augment Headcount**, **Consolidate Tech Stack with AI**, and **Scale Impact Without Overhead**. These firms need to balance client-facing creative deliverables with internal operational efficiency while managing complex talent acquisition workflows.

## Use Case 1: Automated Employer Brand Design System

### Pain
HR/Staffing firms spend 15-20 hours per client creating custom employer brand packages (logos, color schemes, brand guidelines, social media templates). Design teams are bottlenecked with repetitive brand work while strategic creative projects get delayed. Client approval cycles stretch 2-3 weeks due to manual feedback loops.

### Solution
**monday.com Work Management** + **AI Agents** + **Vibe**
- Automated brand generation using Vibe AI prompts based on client industry/culture inputs
- Work Management boards track brand asset production with automated handoffs
- AI Agents generate initial brand concepts, color palettes, and typography suggestions
- Sidekick handles client feedback collection and revision tracking

### Outcome
- **70% reduction** in brand creation time (15 hours → 4.5 hours)
- **Replace 1.5 junior designers** with AI-augmented workflow
- **3x faster** client approval cycles through automated feedback loops
- **Scale to 50+ clients** without adding design headcount

### Discovery Questions
- "How many hours does your team spend creating employer brand packages for each new client?"
- "What's your current client capacity for brand work, and how often do you turn down projects?"
- "How do you currently handle brand guideline consistency across multiple client touchpoints?"
- "What percentage of your design time is spent on repetitive brand elements vs. strategic creative work?"

### Industry Context
HR/Staffing firms increasingly compete on employer brand sophistication. Clients expect professional brand packages that rival in-house marketing teams, but most staffing firms lack dedicated brand designers. The pressure to deliver high-quality creative work while maintaining recruitment efficiency creates resource allocation challenges.

### Vibe Prompt
```
Create a comprehensive employer brand system for [CLIENT_INDUSTRY] company that emphasizes [CLIENT_VALUES]. Include: modern logo concepts, professional color palette (primary, secondary, accent colors), typography stack, social media templates, and brand voice guidelines. Style should be [MODERN/TRADITIONAL/CREATIVE] and appeal to [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC] candidates. Generate 3 distinct visual directions with mood boards.
```

### Agent Blueprint
**Brand Strategy AI Agent**
- **Inputs**: Client intake form, industry research, competitor analysis
- **Processing**: Generate brand concepts, validate against industry standards, create asset variations
- **Outputs**: Brand package, style guide, implementation timeline
- **Handoffs**: Design review board, client approval workflow, asset production queue

## Industry Terminology

**Creative & Design in HR/Staffing Context:**

- **Employer Brand Package**: Complete visual identity system for client companies including logos, colors, typography, and brand guidelines
- **Candidate Experience Touchpoints**: All visual elements candidates encounter during recruitment process
- **Talent Attraction Collateral**: Marketing materials designed to attract and engage potential candidates
- **Recruitment Marketing**: Strategic use of marketing principles and creative content to attract talent
- **Visual Employer Value Proposition (EVP)**: Graphic representation of what makes a company attractive to employees
- **Candidate Journey Mapping**: Visual documentation of all steps in the recruitment and hiring process
- **Talent Pipeline Visual**: Infographic showing candidate flow through different stages of recruitment
- **Employer Brand Audit**: Assessment of visual consistency across all company touchpoints
- **Creative Brief Template**: Standardized format for capturing client creative requirements
- **Brand Compliance Guidelines**: Rules ensuring consistent visual presentation across all materials
