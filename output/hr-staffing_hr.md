## Industry Terminology

**Key Terms:**
- **Ramp Time**: Period required for new recruiter to reach full productivity
- **Fill Ratio**: Percentage of job orders successfully filled with placed candidates
- **Gross Margin**: Revenue minus direct placement costs (contractor pay, benefits)
- **Bill Rate**: Amount charged to client per hour for temporary staff
- **Pay Rate**: Amount paid to temporary employee per hour
- **Markup**: Difference between bill rate and pay rate
- **Submittal-to-Interview Ratio**: Efficiency metric for candidate quality
- **Time-to-Fill**: Average days from job order receipt to candidate start
- **Candidate Falloff**: Rate at which candidates drop from recruitment process
- **Consultant**: Internal term for recruiters in some organizations
- **Desk**: Individual recruiter's portfolio of clients and candidates
- **Producer**: High-performing recruiter who consistently meets targets
- **Perm Placement**: Full-time, permanent job placements (vs. temporary)
- **Contract-to-Hire**: Temporary assignment with potential for permanent conversion
