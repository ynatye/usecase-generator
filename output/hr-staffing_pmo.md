## Industry Terminology

### Core PMO Terms
- **MSP (Managed Service Provider)** - Outsourced talent acquisition program
- **RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing)** - Complete recruitment function outsourcing
- **VMS (Vendor Management System)** - Platform managing contingent workforce suppliers
- **SOW (Statement of Work)** - Project-based work arrangements
- **Fill Rate** - Percentage of open positions successfully filled
- **Time-to-Fill** - Days from job requisition to candidate start date
- **Markup/Margin** - Difference between bill rate and pay rate

### Compliance & Risk
- **SOC2 Type II** - Security and availability controls audit
- **GDPR** - European data protection regulation
- **CCPA** - California Consumer Privacy Act
- **OFCCP** - Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs
- **EEO-1** - Employment diversity reporting requirement
- **Form I-9** - Employment eligibility verification
- **Drug Free Workplace** - Federal contractor requirement

### Technology & Systems
- **ATS (Applicant Tracking System)** - Recruitment workflow platform
- **HRIS (Human Resources Information System)** - HR data management platform
- **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)** - Client interaction tracking
- **API Integration** - System-to-system data connectivity
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)** - Unified authentication across platforms
- **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)** - Data migration process
