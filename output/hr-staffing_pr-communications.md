# monday.com SE Playbook: HR & Staffing × PR & Communications

## Executive Summary

HR & Staffing firms face unique PR & Communications challenges: maintaining employer brand reputation across multiple client accounts, managing crisis communications for workplace incidents involving placed workers, and scaling thought leadership content while juggling internal communications across distributed branch networks. This playbook demonstrates how monday.com's integrated platform replaces fragmented PR tools, consolidates communications workflows, and scales impact through AI-powered content creation and stakeholder management.

**Key Value Drivers:**
1. **Replace or Radically Augment Headcount** - AI Agents handle routine PR tasks, content creation, and stakeholder outreach
2. **Consolidate Tech Stack with AI** - Unified platform replaces 5-8 separate PR/comms tools
3. **Scale Impact Without Overhead** - Standardized processes enable rapid expansion without proportional staff increases

## Industry Terminology

**Candidate Attraction Strategy** - Comprehensive approach to drawing qualified candidates through employer branding and market positioning

**Contingency Staffing** - Recruitment model where fees are earned only upon successful candidate placement

**Direct Sourcing** - Proactive candidate identification and engagement independent of job postings

**Employer Value Proposition (EVP)** - Unique combination of benefits, culture, and opportunities that attract and retain talent

**Fill Rate** - Percentage of open positions successfully filled within specified timeframes

**Passive Candidate Engagement** - Strategies for attracting candidates not actively job searching

**Retained Search** - Premium recruitment service with upfront fees for executive and specialized roles

**Talent Community Building** - Long-term relationship development with potential candidates

**Time-to-Fill** - Average duration from job order receipt to candidate start date

**Workforce Solutions** - Comprehensive talent management services beyond traditional staffing
