## Industry Terminology

### Core Procurement Terms
- **Vendor Management System (VMS)** - Platform managing multiple staffing vendors for large enterprises
- **Master Service Agreement (MSA)** - Umbrella contract covering multiple statements of work
- **Statement of Work (SOW)** - Specific project or service requirements under MSA
- **Service Level Agreement (SLA)** - Performance standards and metrics for vendor accountability
- **Request for Proposal (RFP)** - Formal vendor selection and evaluation process
- **Preferred Vendor List (PVL)** - Pre-approved vendors with negotiated terms and pricing

### HR & Staffing Specific
- **Cost Per Hire (CPH)** - Total recruitment costs divided by number of placements
- **Time to Fill** - Days from job requisition to accepted offer
- **Fill Rate** - Percentage of job orders successfully filled
- **Candidate Quality Score** - Metric measuring placement longevity and performance
- **Bill Rate** - Amount charged to client per hour/day for placed candidate
- **Pay Rate** - Amount paid to candidate per hour/day
- **Gross Margin** - Difference between bill rate and pay rate plus costs

### Technology & Compliance
- **Applicant Tracking System (ATS)** - Software managing candidate pipeline and hiring workflow
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)** - Platform managing client relationships and sales pipeline
- **Employer of Record (EOR)** - Service handling payroll, benefits, and compliance for placed workers
- **Background Check Authorization** - Legal permission for screening potential placements
- **I-9 Verification** - Federal form confirming employment eligibility
- **Drug Testing Clearance** - Required screening for safety-sensitive positions
