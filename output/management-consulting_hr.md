## Industry Terminology

- **Up-or-Out**: Promotion-based career model where consultants advance or leave
- **Engagement**: Client project or assignment
- **Utilization**: Percentage of time spent on billable client work
- **Pipeline**: Prospective candidates or business opportunities
- **Secondment**: Temporary assignment to client organization
- **Practice Area**: Specialized consulting domain (e.g., Strategy, Operations, Technology)
- **Case Team**: Project team assigned to specific client engagement
- **Alumni**: Former consultants who maintain relationships with firm
- **Partner Track**: Career pathway toward firm ownership/partnership
- **Bench Time**: Non-billable periods between engagements
