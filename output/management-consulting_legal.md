# monday.com SE Playbook: Management Consulting × Legal

## Executive Summary

Management consulting firms face unique legal challenges that traditional law firms don't encounter. They must manage complex engagement structures, navigate conflicts across multiple clients, ensure IP protection in deliverables, and maintain compliance across diverse regulatory environments. This playbook addresses how monday.com can transform legal operations within consulting firms, focusing on three key value drivers: replacing legal headcount with AI-powered workflows, consolidating fragmented legal tech stacks, and scaling legal impact without proportional overhead growth.

## Industry Terminology

**Engagement Letter**: Contract defining scope, terms, and deliverables for consulting services
**MSA (Master Service Agreement)**: Framework contract covering multiple engagements with standardized terms
**Conflicts Screen**: Process to identify potential conflicts of interest before accepting new clients
**Information Barrier/Chinese Wall**: Procedures to prevent information sharing between competing client teams
**Work Product**: Deliverables, analysis, and recommendations created during consulting engagements
**Professional Liability**: Insurance covering errors, omissions, and professional negligence
**Unauthorized Practice**: Providing services that require professional licensing (law, accounting, etc.)
**Expert Network**: Third-party services providing access to industry specialists and former executives
**Subcontractor Agreement**: Contract with specialized firms providing services within consulting engagement
**IP Indemnification**: Protection against intellectual property infringement claims
**Regulatory Advisory**: Consulting services related to compliance with government regulations
**Transformation Risk**: Liability exposure from large-scale organizational change initiatives
