# monday.com SE Playbook: Management Consulting × Marketing

## Executive Summary

This playbook targets marketing functions within management consulting firms, focusing on three core value drivers: **Replace/Radically Augment Headcount**, **Consolidate Tech Stack with AI**, and **Scale Impact Without Overhead**. The consulting industry's marketing challenges are unique—from thought leadership content production to managing complex partner ecosystems—requiring specialized workflow orchestration and AI-powered automation.

- **ROI Invisibility**: Can't track which content drives pipeline or strengthens positioning

### Solution
**monday.com Work Management + AI Agents + Vibe**

- **Approval Workflows**: Automated routing to practice leaders and brand compliance
- **Performance Dashboard**: Connect content metrics to CRM pipeline data via mondayDB

### Outcome
- **3x content velocity** with same headcount
- **50% reduction** in partner time per article
- **Measurable ROI**: Track content → leads → deals conversion

BCG's insights, McKinsey's research, and Deloitte's trend reports aren't just marketing; they're business development tools worth millions.

## Industry Terminology

**Key Terms Consulting Marketers Must Know:**

- **Thought Leadership**: Content that positions the firm as an industry expert and trusted advisor
- **Practice Area**: Specialized service line (e.g., Strategy, Operations, Technology, HR)
- **Engagement**: Individual client project or long-term client relationship
- **Proposal/RFP Response**: Formal response to client Request for Proposal
- **Case Study/Client Story**: Success story showcasing project results and firm capabilities
- **Alumni Network**: Former employees who may become clients, referral sources, or re-hires
- **Partner**: Senior-level consultant with client relationship and business development responsibility
- **SME (Subject Matter Expert)**: Consultant with deep expertise in specific industry or functional area
- **Analyst Relations**: Relationship management with industry research firms (Gartner, Forrester, etc.)
- **Co-marketing/Alliance Marketing**: Joint marketing activities with technology or service partners
- **Business Development (BD)**: Sales and relationship management activities
- **Origination**: Generating new client opportunities and engagements
- **Pipeline**: Tracked opportunities from initial interest through closed deals
- **Realization Rate**: Percentage of proposals that result in won business
- **Utilization Rate**: Percentage of consultant time that is billable to clients
- **Practice Development**: Building expertise and market presence in specific service areas
