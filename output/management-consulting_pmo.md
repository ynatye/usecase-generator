# monday.com SE Playbook: Management Consulting × PMO

## Executive Summary

Management consulting PMOs are the operational backbone of complex, multi-million dollar engagements. They orchestrate everything from partner-level portfolio dashboards to granular workstream dependencies while maintaining methodology compliance and client satisfaction. This playbook targets the three critical value drivers: **replacing/augmenting headcount**, **consolidating tech stacks with AI**, and **scaling impact without overhead**.

## Industry Terminology

**Engagement:** A client project or consulting assignment
**Workstream:** A specific track of work within a larger engagement
**Utilization:** Percentage of consultant time that is billable to clients
**Realization:** Actual revenue collected vs. standard billing rates
**Leverage:** Ratio of junior to senior staff on engagements
**Bench Time:** Non-billable time when consultants are between assignments
**QBR:** Quarterly Business Review with clients
**Steering Committee:** Executive oversight group for large engagements
**Gate Review:** Methodology checkpoint before proceeding to next phase
**PMO:** Project Management Office
**SOW:** Statement of Work defining engagement scope and deliverables
**MSA:** Master Service Agreement governing client relationship
**P&L:** Profit & Loss responsibility for engagement financial performance
