## Industry Terminology

- **Thought Leadership**: Strategic content positioning partners as industry experts to drive business development
- **Engagement**: Client project or consulting assignment
- **Pipeline**: Qualified business development opportunities with potential revenue value
- **Partner**: Senior consulting professional with client relationship and business development responsibility
- **Managing Director**: Senior partner with P&L responsibility for practice or geography
- **Principal**: Senior consultant typically 1-2 levels below partner
- **Practice**: Functional or industry specialty area (e.g., Operations, Healthcare, Financial Services)
- **Origination**: Business development activities that create new client relationships
- **Utilization**: Percentage of time consultants spend on billable client work
- **Realization**: Percentage of standard billing rates actually collected from clients
- **Pursuit**: Active business development opportunity with defined scope and timeline
- **RFP Response**: Formal proposal in response to client request for services
- **Case Team**: Project team assigned to specific client engagement
- **Alumni Network**: Former employees now in client organizations or industry positions
- **Secondment**: Consultant temporarily assigned to work within client organization
- **Thought Partnership**: Strategic advisory relationship beyond traditional project scope
