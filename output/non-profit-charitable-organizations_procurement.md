## Industry Terminology

**2 CFR Part 200**: Federal regulation governing procurement standards for federal grant recipients
**Cost-Plus Contract**: Contract type where vendor is reimbursed for costs plus agreed-upon profit
**Diverse Supplier**: Vendors certified as minority-owned, women-owned, veteran-owned, or disadvantaged business enterprises
**GPO (Group Purchasing Organization)**: Cooperative buying arrangement among multiple organizations
**IRS Form 8283**: Required for non-cash charitable contributions over $500
**Micro-Purchase**: Federal procurement threshold currently set at $10,000
**NICRA**: Negotiated Indirect Cost Rate Agreement with federal government
**Program-Related Investment (PRI)**: Investment made primarily to accomplish charitable purposes
**Single Audit**: Annual audit required for non-profits expending $750,000+ in federal awards
**Uniform Guidance**: Federal regulations (2 CFR Part 200) governing grants and cooperative agreements
