## Industry Terminology

**Theory of Change**: Logic model connecting activities to intended outcomes through measurable assumptions
**M&E Framework**: Monitoring and Evaluation system measuring program outputs, outcomes, and impacts
**Evidence-Based Practice**: Interventions proven effective through rigorous research and evaluation
**Implementation Science**: Study of methods to promote uptake of research findings into routine practice
**Logic Model**: Visual representation of program theory showing inputs, activities, outputs, and outcomes
**Impact Measurement**: Quantifiable assessment of social change attributable to program interventions
**Beneficiary**: Individual or community served by non-profit programs (also: client, participant, constituent)
**Stakeholder Mapping**: Identification and analysis of all parties affected by or influencing programs
**Program Fidelity**: Degree to which interventions are implemented as originally designed
**Social Return on Investment (SROI)**: Methodology measuring social, environmental, and economic value created
**Capacity Building**: Process of strengthening abilities of individuals, organizations, and systems
**Systems Change**: Altering underlying structures and relationships that create social problems
