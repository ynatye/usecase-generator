## Industry Terminology

### Regulatory & Compliance
- **MLR (Medical, Legal, Regulatory)**: Cross-functional review committee for promotional materials
- **Fair Balance**: Required presentation of risk information alongside efficacy claims
- **ISI (Important Safety Information)**: Key safety data that must be prominently displayed
- **PI (Prescribing Information)**: Complete product labeling information
- **FDA 21 CFR Part 202**: FDA regulations governing prescription drug advertising
- **OPDP (Office of Prescription Drug Promotion)**: FDA division overseeing promotional compliance
- **DDMAC**: Former name for OPDP, still commonly used
- **Promotional Piece**: Any marketing material intended for external audiences
- **Non-Promotional**: Materials that don't promote specific products (educational only)

### Creative & Production
- **Detail Aid**: Sales presentation materials used by pharmaceutical representatives
- **Leave-Behind**: Materials left with healthcare providers after sales calls
- **HCP (Healthcare Provider/Professional)**: Target audience for professional materials
- **KOL (Key Opinion Leader)**: Influential physicians who shape treatment patterns
- **MOA (Mechanism of Action)**: How a drug works biologically
- **Real-World Evidence (RWE)**: Clinical evidence from actual patient experience
- **Patient Journey**: End-to-end patient experience from diagnosis to treatment
- **Adherence**: Patient compliance with prescribed treatment regimens
- **Health Literacy**: Patient ability to understand and act on health information

### Commercial & Strategy
- **Launch Excellence**: Optimized new product introduction processes
- **Brand Strategy**: Overall positioning and messaging approach for pharmaceutical products
- **Lifecycle Management**: Strategy for managing products throughout their commercial lifecycle
- **Market Access**: Strategies for obtaining payer coverage and reimbursement
- **Medical Communications**: Scientific communication with healthcare audiences
- **HEOR (Health Economics and Outcomes Research)**: Economic value demonstration of treatments
- **Value Proposition**: Core benefit statement for pharmaceutical products
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Analysis of competitor activities and strategies
