## Industry Terminology

### Key Terms
- **PSP (Patient Support Program)**: Comprehensive support services helping patients access and stay on therapy
- **Hub Services**: Centralized coordination center managing specialty pharmacy network and patient flow
- **HCP (Healthcare Provider)**: Physicians, nurse practitioners, and other prescribing clinicians
- **Prior Authorization (PA)**: Payer requirement for pre-approval before covering specific medications
- **Specialty Pharmacy**: Pharmacies specializing in complex, high-cost medications requiring special handling
- **Formulary**: List of medications covered by insurance plans with associated coverage levels
- **HEOR (Health Economics & Outcomes Research)**: Analysis demonstrating medication value and cost-effectiveness
- **Real-World Evidence (RWE)**: Clinical evidence derived from real-world data sources outside clinical trials
- **Patient Journey Mapping**: Visual representation of patient experience from diagnosis through treatment
- **Persistence**: Duration of therapy measured from initiation to discontinuation
- **Adherence**: Extent to which patients take medication as prescribed (typically measured as % of days covered)
- **Copay Cards**: Manufacturer assistance reducing patient out-of-pocket costs
- **Bridge Therapy**: Temporary medication supply while awaiting insurance approval or specialty pharmacy fulfillment

### Regulatory Context
- **HIPAA**: Healthcare privacy regulations governing patient information handling
- **FDA Guidance**: Regulatory requirements for pharmaceutical company communications and support programs
- **Anti-Kickback Statute**: Regulations preventing inappropriate inducements in healthcare
- **Sunshine Act**: Transparency requirements for payments and transfers of value to healthcare providers
