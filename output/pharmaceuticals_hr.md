## Industry Terminology

**Core HR Terms:**
- **GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices):** FDA-regulated manufacturing standards
- **GCP (Good Clinical Practice):** Standards for clinical trial conduct
- **IND/CTA:** Investigational New Drug applications for clinical trials
- **CMC (Chemistry, Manufacturing, Controls):** Drug development regulatory track
- **CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action):** Quality management processes
- **SOX Compliance:** Financial reporting requirements for public companies
- **21 CFR Part 11:** Electronic records and signatures regulations

**Scientific Roles:**
- **Principal Investigator (PI):** Lead clinical trial researcher
- **CRO (Contract Research Organization):** Outsourced clinical trial services
- **MSL (Medical Science Liaison):** Scientific field-based medical affairs role
- **CMO (Contract Manufacturing Organization):** Outsourced manufacturing services
- **HEOR:** Health Economics and Outcomes Research
- **PK/PD:** Pharmacokinetics/Pharmacodynamics specialists

**Regulatory & Compliance:**
- **Pharmacovigilance:** Drug safety monitoring and adverse event reporting
- **QP (Qualified Person):** EU regulatory role for batch release
- **REMS (Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy):** FDA safety requirements
- **PAES (Post-authorization Efficacy Study):** EU post-market studies
- **ICH Guidelines:** International harmonized drug development standards
