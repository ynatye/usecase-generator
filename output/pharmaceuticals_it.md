## Industry Terminology

### Regulatory & Compliance Terms
- **21 CFR Part 11**: FDA regulation governing electronic records and signatures
- **ALCOA+**: Data integrity principles (Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate + Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available)
- **CSV (Computer System Validation)**: Process of establishing documented evidence that a computer system does what it's supposed to do
- **GxP**: Good Practice regulations (GMP, GCP, GLP, etc.)
- **IQ/OQ/PQ**: Installation/Operational/Performance Qualification phases
- **Data Integrity**: Completeness, consistency, and accuracy of data throughout its lifecycle

### Clinical & Laboratory Systems
- **LIMS**: Laboratory Information Management System
- **EDC**: Electronic Data Capture system for clinical trials
- **CTMS**: Clinical Trial Management System
- **eTMF**: Electronic Trial Master File
- **RTSM**: Randomization and Trial Supply Management
- **ELN**: Electronic Laboratory Notebook

### Manufacturing & Quality Systems
- **MES**: Manufacturing Execution System
- **QMS**: Quality Management System
- **Batch Record**: Documentation of manufacturing processes for each product batch
- **Deviation**: Departure from approved procedures or specifications
- **CAPA**: Corrective and Preventive Action process
