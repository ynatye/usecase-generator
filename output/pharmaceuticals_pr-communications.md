## Industry Terminology

### Core Terms
- **REMS (Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy):** FDA-required risk management plans for certain medications
- **IND (Investigational New Drug):** FDA application to begin human clinical trials
- **NDA/BLA (New Drug Application/Biologics License Application):** FDA submissions for drug approval
- **PDUFA (Prescription Drug User Fee Act):** FDA review timeline framework
- **PhRMA:** Pharmaceutical Research and Manufacturers of America trade association
- **ICH Guidelines:** International Council for Harmonisation regulatory standards
- **Pharmacovigilance:** Post-market safety monitoring and adverse event management
- **KOL (Key Opinion Leader):** Influential healthcare professionals who shape treatment practices
- **MSL (Medical Science Liaison):** Bridge between pharma companies and healthcare professionals
- **Therapeutic Area:** Disease-focused business units (oncology, cardiovascular, neuroscience, etc.)

### Communications-Specific Terms
- **Embargo:** Restriction on publishing information until specified time/date
- **Dear Doctor Letter:** Official safety communication sent to healthcare providers
- **Risk Communication:** Messaging about drug risks and benefits
- **Medical Information:** Scientific inquiry response and educational support
- **Advisory Board:** Expert panel providing strategic guidance on development/commercialization
- **Investigator Meeting:** Training sessions for clinical trial researchers
- **Medical Writing:** Creation of regulatory and scientific documents
- **Publication Planning:** Strategic approach to disseminating clinical data
- **Scientific Platform:** Core therapeutic messaging framework
- **Launch Sequence:** Coordinated market introduction timeline and activities
