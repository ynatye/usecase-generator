## Industry Terminology

**21 CFR Part 11**: FDA regulation governing electronic records and signatures
**API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient)**: The biologically active component of a drug
**CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action)**: Quality management system for addressing deviations
**CMC (Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls)**: Drug development regulatory section
**CMO (Contract Manufacturing Organization)**: Third-party pharmaceutical manufacturer
**CRO (Contract Research Organization)**: Third-party clinical research provider
**CSV (Computer System Validation)**: Process ensuring systems meet regulatory requirements
**GLP (Good Laboratory Practice)**: Quality standards for non-clinical laboratory studies
**GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice)**: Quality standards for pharmaceutical manufacturing
**GxP**: Collective term for Good Practice quality guidelines (GLP, GMP, GCP, etc.)
**LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System)**: Software managing laboratory data
**MES (Manufacturing Execution System)**: System controlling manufacturing operations
**OOS (Out of Specification)**: Results falling outside established acceptance criteria
**PIC/S**: International pharmaceutical inspection cooperation scheme
**QbD (Quality by Design)**: Systematic approach to pharmaceutical development
