## Industry Terminology

### Core Sustainability Terms
- **API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient)** - The biologically active component of a drug
- **Green Chemistry** - Design of chemical products and processes that reduce or eliminate hazardous substances
- **Atom Economy** - Percentage of starting materials incorporated into final product
- **E-Factor** - Environmental factor measuring waste generated per kg of product
- **Mass Intensity** - Total mass input divided by product mass output
- **Process Mass Intensity (PMI)** - Comprehensive measure including solvents, reagents, and water

### Regulatory & Compliance
- **ICH Q3D** - Guideline on elemental impurities in pharmaceuticals
- **FDA Green Chemistry Program** - Initiative promoting sustainable pharmaceutical manufacturing
- **EU REACH** - Registration, Evaluation, Authorization of Chemicals regulation
- **RCRA** - Resource Conservation and Recovery Act governing hazardous waste
- **GxP** - Good Practice guidelines (GMP, GLP, GCP) with sustainability considerations

### Carbon & Climate
- **Scope 3 Emissions** - Indirect emissions from value chain (often 70-80% of pharma carbon footprint)
- **Science-Based Targets (SBTi)** - Emission reduction targets aligned with climate science
- **Carbon Intensity** - CO2 emissions per unit of revenue or production
- **Cold Chain** - Temperature-controlled supply chain for pharmaceuticals

### Manufacturing & Operations
- **CRO/CMO** - Contract Research/Manufacturing Organization
- **cGMP** - Current Good Manufacturing Practices
- **PAT (Process Analytical Technology)** - Framework for pharmaceutical process monitoring
- **QbD (Quality by Design)** - Systematic approach to pharmaceutical development
