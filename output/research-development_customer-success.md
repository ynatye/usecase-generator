# monday.com SE Playbook: Research & Development × Customer Success

## Executive Summary

Research & Development organizations face unique customer success challenges that traditional CS tools can't address. From managing complex relationships with research sponsors and industry partners to ensuring satisfaction across shared core facilities and technology licensees, R&D customer success requires specialized workflows that understand the nuanced, long-term, and highly technical nature of research relationships.

This playbook demonstrates how monday.com's integrated platform transforms R&D customer success operations by consolidating fragmented tools, automating relationship management at scale, and providing AI-powered insights that replace traditional headcount-intensive approaches.

**Key Value Drivers:**
1. **Replace or Radically Augment Headcount** - AI-powered relationship management and automated workflows
2. **Consolidate Tech Stack with AI** - Unified platform replacing 5-12 disparate tools
3. **Scale Impact Without Overhead** - Systematic approach to managing complex R&D stakeholder relationships

## Industry Terminology

### Key Terms
- **Principal Investigator (PI)**: Lead researcher responsible for grant management and research execution
- **Core Facility**: Shared research resource providing specialized equipment and expertise
- **Technology Transfer Office (TTO)**: Unit responsible for commercializing research discoveries
- **Clinical Research Associate (CRA)**: Professional responsible for clinical trial site management
- **Institutional Review Board (IRB)**: Ethics committee overseeing research involving human subjects
- **Sponsored Research Agreement (SRA)**: Contract governing industry-sponsored research
- **Material Transfer Agreement (MTA)**: Agreement governing sharing of research materials
- **Intellectual Property (IP) Disclosure**: Formal notification of potentially patentable discoveries

### Acronyms
- **NIH**: National Institutes of Health
- **NSF**: National Science Foundation
- **FDA**: Food and Drug Administration
- **GCP**: Good Clinical Practice
- **CRADA**: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement
- **SBIR/STTR**: Small Business Innovation Research/Small Business Technology Transfer
- **FTE**: Full-Time Equivalent
- **ROI**: Return on Investment
- **KPI**: Key Performance Indicator
