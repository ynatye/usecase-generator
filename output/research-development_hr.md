## Industry Terminology

**Principal Investigator (PI):** Lead researcher responsible for a research project or grant, typically PhD-level with team management responsibilities.

**Postdoc/Postdoctoral Researcher:** PhD holder pursuing additional research training, typically 2-5 year positions before permanent roles.

**Visiting Scholar:** Researcher from another institution temporarily collaborating, often on sabbatical.

**Research Staff Scientist:** Permanent research position, typically not tenure-track, focused on project execution.

**Academic Appointment:** Formal position structure often borrowed from universities (Assistant/Associate/Full Scientist levels).

**Sabbatical:** Extended leave (typically 6-12 months) for research or professional development, common in academic-adjacent roles.

**H-1B/O-1/J-1 Visas:** Common visa categories for international researchers (specialty occupation, extraordinary ability, exchange visitor).

**IRB (Institutional Review Board):** Committee that reviews research involving human subjects for ethical compliance.

**IACUC:** Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee for research involving animals.

**Technology Transfer:** Process of moving research discoveries toward commercial application.

**Conflict of Interest (COI):** Situations where personal interests might compromise research objectivity.

**Grant Compliance:** Adhering to funding agency requirements and reporting obligations.
