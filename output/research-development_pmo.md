## Industry Terminology

**Key Terms:**
- **Principal Investigator (PI)**: Lead researcher responsible for research program execution
- **Co-Investigator (Co-I)**: Collaborating researcher with specific expertise or resources
- **Research Administrator**: Professional managing grant compliance and administrative requirements
- **IRB (Institutional Review Board)**: Ethics committee overseeing human subjects research
- **IACUC (Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee)**: Ethics committee overseeing animal research
- **Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)**: Professional managing clinical trial operations
- **Sponsored Research**: Externally funded research with specific deliverable requirements
- **Technology Transfer**: Process of commercializing research discoveries
- **Research Compliance Officer**: Professional ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements
- **Grant Lifecycle**: End-to-end process from application through closeout
- **Direct Costs**: Research expenses directly attributable to specific projects
- **Indirect Costs (F&A)**: Institutional overhead costs for research support
- **Milestone-Driven Funding**: Funding released based on achievement of specific objectives
- **Good Clinical Practice (GCP)**: International quality standards for clinical trial conduct
- **Research Data Management Plan**: Strategy for data collection, storage, and sharing
- **Publication Pipeline**: Process from research completion to peer-reviewed publication
