## Industry Terminology

**Key Terms R&D Procurement Professionals Must Know:**

- **Sole Source Justification**: Documentation required when only one vendor can meet technical requirements
- **Cost Sharing**: Grant requirement to contribute organizational funds matching federal funding
- **Chain of Custody**: Complete documentation of controlled material handling from receipt to disposal
- **Statement of Work (SOW)**: Detailed description of services to be performed by CROs or service providers
- **Master Service Agreement (MSA)**: Umbrella contract governing multiple projects with a single vendor
- **Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP)**: Quality standards required for pharmaceutical and biotechnology materials
- **Animal Welfare Compliance**: Regulatory requirements for research animal procurement and care (IACUC)
- **Technology Transfer**: Process for commercializing research innovations requiring specialized IP management
- **Clinical Trial Supplies**: Specialized procurement for investigational drugs and medical devices
- **Biosafety Level Classification**: Security categorization affecting procurement and handling of biological materials
