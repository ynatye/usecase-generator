## Industry Terminology

**Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)**: 9-level scale measuring technology maturity from basic research (TRL 1) to full commercial deployment (TRL 9)

**Stage-Gate Process**: Structured approach to innovation management with defined gates where projects are evaluated for continuation

**Innovation Pipeline**: Portfolio view of research and development projects at various stages of maturity

**Proof-of-Concept (POC)**: Early demonstration that a research idea has practical potential

**Minimum Viable Product (MVP)**: Simplest version of a product that provides value and enables learning

**Patent Landscape**: Comprehensive view of intellectual property in a specific technology domain

**Prior Art**: Existing knowledge that may affect the patentability of an invention

**Freedom to Operate (FTO)**: Legal right to commercialize a product without infringing existing patents

**Technology Transfer**: Process of moving research discoveries from laboratory to commercial application

**Cross-Functional Teams**: Multidisciplinary groups combining different expertise areas for research projects

**Knowledge Graph**: Network representation of interconnected research insights and relationships

**Competitive Intelligence**: Systematic collection and analysis of competitor activities and strategies

**IP Portfolio**: Collection of intellectual property assets owned by an organization

**Research Infrastructure**: Physical and digital resources supporting scientific investigation

**Institutional Knowledge**: Collective expertise and insights accumulated within an organization
