## Industry Terminology

### Core Terms
- **Technology Transfer Office (TTO)**: University department responsible for commercializing research discoveries
- **Sponsored Research Agreement (SRA)**: Contract between industry sponsor and research institution for specific research services
- **Material Transfer Agreement (MTA)**: Legal document governing transfer of research materials between organizations
- **Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA)**: Partnership between government laboratories and private industry
- **Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR)**: Federal program providing R&D funding to small businesses
- **Freedom to Operate (FTO)**: Analysis confirming technology can be commercialized without infringing existing patents

### Financial & Legal Terms
- **Indirect Cost Rate**: University overhead percentage applied to direct costs in sponsored research
- **F&A (Facilities & Administrative)**: Federal terminology for indirect costs in government-sponsored research
- **Milestone Payments**: Licensing revenue tied to specific development or commercial achievements
- **Minimum Annual Royalties**: Guaranteed licensing payments regardless of actual product sales
- **Patent Prosecution**: Process of obtaining patent protection through patent office examination
- **Prior Art**: Existing knowledge that may affect patent validity or scope

### Regulatory & Compliance
- **Institutional Review Board (IRB)**: Committee reviewing human subjects research for ethical compliance
- **Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC)**: Committee overseeing animal research protocols
- **Export Administration Regulations (EAR)**: Federal rules governing export of dual-use technologies
- **Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)**: Treasury department enforcing economic sanctions
- **Conflict of Interest (COI)**: Situations where personal interests may bias research objectivity
