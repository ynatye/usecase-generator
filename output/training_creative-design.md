## Industry Context & Terminology

### Key Industry Terms
- **SCORM/xAPI**: Technical standards for eLearning content packaging and tracking
- **LXP (Learning Experience Platform)**: Modern learner-centric platforms vs traditional LMS
- **Microlearning**: Bite-sized content typically 3-7 minutes for just-in-time learning
- **ILT (Instructor-Led Training)**: Traditional classroom or virtual instructor delivery
- **Blended Learning**: Combination of digital and in-person training modalities
- **Kirkpatrick Model**: Four-level framework for evaluating training effectiveness
- **ADDIE**: Instructional design methodology (Analyze, Design, Develop, Implement, Evaluate)
- **Gamification**: Game elements applied to learning to increase engagement
- **Adaptive Learning**: AI-powered personalization of learning paths based on performance

### Stakeholder Roles
- **Chief Learning Officer (CLO)**: Strategic learning vision and ROI accountability
- **Learning & Development (L&D) Director**: Operational program management and team leadership
- **Instructional Designer**: Learning experience architecture and pedagogy
- **Content Developer**: Subject matter expertise translation into learning materials
- **Creative Director**: Visual brand and design strategy across all learning materials
- **UX Designer**: Learner interface optimization and user experience research
- **Motion Graphics Artist**: Video and interactive content animation
- **Technical Writer**: Documentation and written learning content creation
- **Learning Technology Manager**: Platform integration and technical implementation
- **Quality Assurance Specialist**: Content accuracy and compliance verification

### Adjacent Departments
- **Marketing**: Brand consistency and learner acquisition campaigns
- **Product Management**: Feature requirements for internal learning products
- **Human Resources**: Employee onboarding and compliance training coordination
- **Sales Enablement**: Customer-facing training content and sales support materials
- **Customer Success**: User education and adoption training programs
- **IT/Technical Writing**: Documentation and software training content
- **Compliance**: Regulatory training requirements and audit preparation

### Competitive Landscape
- **Traditional LMS**: Moodle, Blackboard, Canvas - established but outdated

- **Design Tools**: Figma, Canva, Adobe Creative Suite - creative workflow tools
