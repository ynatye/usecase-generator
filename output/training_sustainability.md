## Industry Terminology

### Environmental & Sustainability Terms
- **Carbon Footprint**: Total greenhouse gas emissions from training activities (scope 1, 2, 3)
- **ESG Frameworks**: Environmental, Social, Governance reporting standards (GRI, SASB, TCFD)
- **Science-Based Targets (SBTs)**: Emissions reduction goals aligned with climate science
- **Circular Economy**: Resource optimization minimizing waste and maximizing reuse
- **Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)**: Comprehensive environmental impact analysis
- **Net Zero**: Achieving balance between emissions produced and removed
- **Scope 1/2/3 Emissions**: Direct, indirect energy, and value chain emissions
- **Green Certifications**: LEED, BREEAM, ENERGY STAR facility standards
- **Sustainable Procurement**: Vendor selection based on environmental and social criteria

### Training Industry Terms
- **Learning Management System (LMS)**: Platform for delivering and tracking training
- **Competency Framework**: Structured approach to skill development and assessment
- **Blended Learning**: Combination of online and in-person training methods
- **Microlearning**: Bite-sized learning modules for just-in-time skill development
- **Learning Analytics**: Data analysis of training effectiveness and learner behavior
- **Instructional Design**: Systematic approach to creating effective learning experiences
- **Assessment Validity**: Accuracy of measurements in evaluating learning outcomes
- **Training ROI**: Return on investment measurement for learning programs
- **Compliance Training**: Mandatory education to meet regulatory requirements

### Technology & AI Terms
- **Algorithmic Bias**: Systematic errors in AI systems affecting fairness
- **Explainable AI (XAI)**: AI systems that provide transparent decision-making processes
- **Digital Carbon Footprint**: Environmental impact of technology infrastructure
- **Responsible AI**: Ethical frameworks for AI development and deployment
- **Learning Personalization**: Adaptive content based on individual learner needs
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasting learner outcomes and optimization opportunities
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: AI capability for understanding human language
- **Machine Learning Operations (MLOps)**: Practices for deploying AI systems at scale
